欺山趕海踐雪徑也未絕望（十六）

Posted on 2024-08-31 In Journal
Symbols count in article: 2.1k

Sherlock embraces the ache of Eurus’s soul, feels her pain for her.

看Sherlock S4E3，一直看一次哭一次。Eurus最开心的时候，一个是和Sherlock出去半夜压马路，一个是“This time you get it right”。

人不会天生选择“请看我受苦，请看我疼痛，请帮助我”。人天生就会“我只想看到你長得美，但不想知道你在受罪；我想要得到天上的水，但不是你的淚”。Save plot出场来源于“希望被救助”，有时还来源于“希望被特定的人救助”。在面对超过自己能力范围的困难时，fight or flight or freeze or fawn? 长期面对超过自己能力范围的困难时，self destructive plot or secure plot? 成年人可以讲自己熬过的漫漫长夜，可是小孩子还什么都不知道呢！

有时候福至心灵会想到Eurus那句“Which one’s pain?” Do you feel the pain from the one you care about? Do you take it as a metaphor for love? You know what? Everyone explique their care and their pain in different ways. It’s hard to imagine if you never feel it. Also, you may fail to decipher it even if you’ve enncountered it, without the help from someone you could really feel. And may it not be called a dilemma, as may there be someone to help you figure the love things out. And you know what, to be mentally strong also means to feel and express the real emotional flow freely. That’s where Mycroft falls short.

I’ll try to be more specific.

Eurus felt pain in a deeper way; she yearned to confirm her existence. It’s not a self-harm behavior; it’s just something like self-harm till I am reassured of something. And THAT something is that she is still alive, still loved, still feeling this world. But how? Adults have many ways to ensure their existence; they see the world through their own reflection. But for an utterly smart kid? there was only the weight of suffering.

Eurus may be told that her parents and brothers loved her. But how could you say it’s love if it cannot alleviate her pain? Eurus understood it was pain, but without a decade of waiting and wandering, she could not really discern what causes that pain and how to ease it. You see, if nobody recognized her struggle and / or offered help, what’s the point on earth to tell her that she is beloved?

And here comes Sherlock. Unlike Mycroft, Sherlock is always the “grown-up”. Sherlock has the rare ability to connect with Eurus’s emotional landscape. For Sherlock, the thing comes easier: he feels the pain for Eurus, and that’s all. Long long afterward, Eurus is waiting for her brother to feel her internal pain, and respond in a SWEET way.

Here’s something about myself.

大概两年前，我写日记说，“疼痛”这个东西总是不会轻易示人，像野兽。在我分辨不出自己的感受、更不会表达的时候，解离依然是痛的。如果想要寻求心疼或者帮助，我首先会寻求“可以示人的疼痛”：这件事情就变成了，像特定的人展示身体上的疼痛、或是像特定的人寻求“得到疼痛”的过程，变成了U did it, u know。不过好在，所有的事情最后都变成了“I will save myself, someday somehow”，也算还不错的结果：当然知道所有的心上的事情最终都是求于己，但是这个变化的过程非常地有趣，把它们都记录下来我感到很难得。
